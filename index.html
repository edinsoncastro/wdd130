Colombia

Bogotá, the capital of Colombia, is a vibrant city located in the Andean highlands at more than 2,600 meters above sea level. It is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and cool climate. It is notable for its historic center, La Candelaria, with cobblestone streets, museums such as the Gold Museum, and colonial architecture. In addition, Bogotá is an important economic and cultural center, with a varied gastronomy, active nightlife, and green spaces such as Cerro de Monserrate and Parque Simón Bolívar. It is a city that combines tradition and modernity in perfect harmony.
