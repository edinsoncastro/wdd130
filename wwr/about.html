Our History

We were born by the river and grew up with it. From our first adventures in a small boat to becoming leaders in rafting in the region, we’ve built a story filled with passion for adventure and love for nature. Founded in 1998, White Water Rafting has provided unforgettable experiences to adventurers from all around the world. With our team of expert guides and top-quality equipment, we guarantee safe, thrilling, and adrenaline-filled adventures. Join our story and experience the excitement of the river like never before!

Our journey is one of overcoming challenges, embracing change, and pushing the limits of what’s possible in the rafting world. Over the years, we’ve expanded to new locations and created lasting memories for thousands of clients. Through it all, our core values of safety, adventure, and sustainability have remained unchanged, ensuring that we continue to provide thrilling experiences while preserving the beauty of nature for future generations.
